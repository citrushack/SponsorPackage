Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the event being held?

The venue is located at the University of California, Riverside. It will be held inside Bourne's College of Engineering. This venue is home to a beautiful courtyard and rooms for all the hackers coding needs.

Why is it named Citrus Hack?

Riverside is known as one of the fore runners in orange making, around the school are thousands of orange trees that are being used for research and to create the most delicious OJ. We wanted our hackathon to promote the unknown beauties of the inland empire, while also expressing its rise in technologic activity.

Who comes to Hackathons?

Hackathons house the brightest and quickest mind of all of the universities around the globe. At a Hackathon you will find the project leaders, the wiz kids, and the problem solvers. In the end, it's a bunch of passionate and talented coders who love to problem solve, build, and create masterpiece all while under the pressure of a thirty-six hour clock.
